About Joseph Ingham

With 38+ years of experience spanning clinical practice, dental education, and dento-legal advisory, Joseph brings unparalleled insight to protecting dental professionals across the UK.

After 28 years in general dental practice and 8 years as Dental Practice Advisor for Berkshire, Joseph spent 10 years with a large mutual defence organisation before joining the British Dental Association as their Dento-Legal Advisor.
